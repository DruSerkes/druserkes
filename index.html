About Me

My name is Dru, and I'm a software engineer skilled in full stack development.
I have experience building RESTful APIs using both Node.js and Python, working with databases and ready-built APIs, and making responsive frontend UIs with React.js.
I am currently seeking work that will allow me to grow and improve as an engineer while contributing my unique perspective and strong work ethic to your company mission.

As a professional Broadway actor for the last 10 years, I've learned that there is always more than one way to do anything, and I grew comfortable with constantly iterating on my performance to improve my work. While my nights were spent on stage everywhere from New York to Las Vegas, I poured hours daily into broadening my knowledge of various fields. It was during this time that I found a joy for software development, and discovered a desire to delve into this career.

I am currently enrolled in Springboards' Software Engineering Career Track to fully realize this transition.
